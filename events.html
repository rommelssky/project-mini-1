EVENTS

At LocalWind Outdoors, we believe that outdoor events are a great way to connect with nature and other outdoor enthusiasts in Quezon Province. Our events are designed to provide visitors with unique and meaningful experiences in the great outdoors, whether it's a coastal cleanup, hiking excursion, camping trip, nature photography workshop, or birdwatching tour.

Our events are led by experienced guides who are knowledgeable about the outdoor destinations in Quezon Province and are committed to promoting sustainable and responsible outdoor recreation. We aim to create a safe and welcoming environment for all participants and to provide them with opportunities to learn new skills, connect with nature, and make lasting memories.

  • Coastal Clean Up
    May 15, 2023

    Join us on May 15th for a coastal cleanup event at the beautiful beaches of Pagbilao. Help us keep the beaches clean and protect the marine life by participating in this meaningful activity.

  • Hiking Excursion
    June 20, 2023

    Take a break from the city and join us on June 20th for a hiking excursion to Mt. Banahaw. This guided hike will take you through breathtaking scenery and challenging terrain, making for an unforgettable outdoor adventure.

  • Camping Under the Stars
    July 10, 2023

    Experience the beauty of nature up close and personal by joining our camping trip on July 10th at the picturesque Tamaraw Falls. This overnight camping trip is the perfect opportunity to disconnect from the busy world and enjoy a night under the stars.
